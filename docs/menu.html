Booking info

As we are a small restaurant, any tables over 10 are now required to pre-order a minimum of 24 hours in advance, and will incur a 10% service charge.

We strongly recommend booking for Friday and Saturday evening as bookings fill up quickly and we hate to turn people away.

Please note: the restaurant becomes very busy 1-2 hours before any Gloucester home rugby games.
