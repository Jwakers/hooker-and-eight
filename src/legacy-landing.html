Collection service

Hooker & Eight now operate collection service. This will run throughout our working week (Weds-Sat).

Pizzas and Calzones will be cooked three quarters of the way through to be finished in your oven at home (180C for 2-3 minutes or to taste).

Collection line will be open from 0930am each day (0600 via online system and 0930 telephone) until 8pm (last orders). Collections will run from 5pm to 8.30pm (last collection) and collection points will be every ten minutes. As always you can park outside of the restaurant from 5pm.

If there is another customer in the restaurant, please wait in your car until your order is ready.
