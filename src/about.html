about us

Set in the heart of Gloucester's city centre, Hooker & Eight bring you their own mouth watering take on neapolitan pizza

Playing homage to Glouceter's rich rugby heritage. The team have refurbished no.49 Westgate street with as much reclaimed material as they could get their hands on, creating a warm relaxed atmosphere for all to enjoy.

It's not just food they pride themselves on! Hooker & Eight also boast a range of craft beers, local ciders and wines from around the globe.
